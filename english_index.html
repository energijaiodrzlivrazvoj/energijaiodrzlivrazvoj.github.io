Useful tips on saving

  • Place thermal insulation on the outside of the walls of your house / apartment.
  • Place heating elements (radiators, stoves) under the windows so that they can heat the air while entering.
  • Well closed oven door while cooking saves energy.
  • Use electric cars - if possible.
  • Simply shower, do not leave the hot water flowing while shampooing.
  • Immerse yourself in a renewable energy source (for electricity generated from solar collectors, wind turbines, etc.)
  • Dress up warmer, rather than extreme warming.
  • Improve thermal insulation on the floor.
  • Fill the gaps on the window frames.
  • Use the express-pots that save energy and time.
